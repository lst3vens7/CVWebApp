Communication

A notable module I chose to undertake in my final year of university was Large-Scale Software Engineering, during this module our small team were given a scenario and asked to complete the full pre-implementation phases for this product. This involved regular team discussions to equally divide work and identify strengths and weaknesses in the group to help maintain an efficient workflow and provide support to any team members that were struggling with a particular task using clear, concise communication, especially as some members of the team were foreign students. The team communicated with multiple in person meetings every week, as well as a weekly scheduled Teams meeting where members could compare work and voice any problems they were having. Because of the groups constant communication and distribution process, we were able to complete the assignment with time to spare, each member gaining a high grade in the process.
